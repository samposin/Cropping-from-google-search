Park Royal - Details

About the Project

Rainbow Housing introduces yet another residential township in Pune. Park Royal, located in Pimple Saudagar, Pune, has been launched by Rainbow Housing in association with Pride Purple. Priced between Rs. 64 lacs and Rs. 1.15 crores (per sq-ft price ranging between Rs. 6213 and Rs. 7812), Park Royal has multi-storey apartments with sets of 2 and 3 BHK premium flats covering areas from 950 sq-ft to 1550 sq-ft. 

Park Royal in Pimple Saudagar is a world-class residential township that is completely self-contained for luxurious and comfortable living. The project offers premium flats in varying sizes that would suit different budgets and requirements. The best part of this township is that each and every apartment of Park Royal has a terrace that overlooks the ever-changing scenic beauty of the Wakad skyline. 

Equipped with all the basic and advanced amenities like power backup, elevators, reserved car parking spaces, swimming pools, security, kids play zone, rentable community area, flower gardens, indoor games room, indoor badminton and squash courts, gymnasium, parks, intercom facilities, private gardens or terraces, and rain water harvesting, Park Royal caters to every need of every resident. Vaastu compliance brings peace of mind to the believers, while an ever-attentive maintenance staff is always ready to serve the residents. Well-connected with the other parts of the city, Park Royal has reputed schools, banks and hospitals nearby.
